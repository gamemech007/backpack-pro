# Backpack Pro

> ⚠️ Worried about losing your items after death? Looking for a vanilla-friendly storage solution?

Backpack Pro adds simple, powerful, and vanilla-friendly backpacks to Minecraft, giving you extra storage without breaking immersion — while also protecting your stored items after death.

But there’s a catch: each time you die, one random item from the backpack is dropped, adding a bit of risk to your survival.

---

# 🎒 Features

## Multiple Backpack Tiers
- 🪨 Iron Backpack
- 💎 Diamond Backpack
- 🖤 Netherite Backpack

## Expandable Storage
- Each tier provides increased inventory space

## Portable Inventory
- Access your backpack anytime with a single key/button

## Durability System
- Backpacks have durability
- ⚠️ Losing durability on death adds a strategic element

## Clean GUI
- Custom-designed interfaces for each tier
- Matches Minecraft’s vanilla style

---

# ⚙️ How to Use
1. Craft a backpack
2. Keep it in your inventory or offhand
3. Press the keybind (default: `B`) or right-click to open
4. Store your items safely
